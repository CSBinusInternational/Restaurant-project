About Us

Bon Appetit is a restaurant that was built in 1995, featuring a menu that is very diversified and offers specialities from different countries in the world. We are happy to open our doors to you and show our decorations that changes depending on the celebration of the day. It started from only french gastronomy and that's why the name "Bon Appetit" which means enjoy your meal in French, but since then it has expanded to different other specialities and now has hired the best chef in the world for the ful pleasure of our clients. We have a lot events that happens during the year with special menu and special decorations, be attentive.
